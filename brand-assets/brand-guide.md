# BetMGM Brand Guide

_Extracted from brand book screenshots, logo pack, and MBR presentation model (Feb 2026)._

---

## Logo

### Structure
- Two elements: **lion icon** (cropped inside a slanted rectangle shape) + **typography** (brand name)
- Two formats available: **Stacked** (Primary) and **Horizontal** (Secondary)
- Stacked is Primary — the icon appears larger on various media
- Spacing between icon and text = double the letter-spacing between letters, in both formats
- Height difference: single spacing in stacked, double in horizontal

## Colours

### Brand Colours
| Colour | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| **Gold** | `#B19661` | 'MGM' text always in gold |
| **Black** | — | Lion icon structure, dark backgrounds |
| **White** | — | 'BET' text on dark backgrounds |

### Colour Rules
- 'MGM' is always in **gold**
- 'BET' is in **white** on dark/toned backgrounds; **black** on light backgrounds
- Lion icon: always black & gold — lion face & shape structure in black; mane, eyes, nose & mouth in black
- The lion's face must always be **lighter** than the mane — never invert icon colours

---

## Logo — Monochrome
- Stacked is the Primary monochrome version
- Horizontal monochrome used only when needed (single colour removes differentiation between the two words)
- White version: dark/toned backgrounds
- Black version: light backgrounds

---

## Logotype (Text Only)
A text-only version (no lion icon) exists for specific situations — use only when minimum size requirements for the primary logo cannot be met.

### Minimum Sizes
| Format | Digital | Print (300ppi) |
|---|---|---|
| Horizontal | 10px H | 3.5mm H |
| Stacked | 20px H | 7mm H |

---

## Lion Icon
- Can be used standalone **only** when used in the same area as a brand name mention — not as a completely isolated asset
- **Do not alter**: no removal or changes to the border, no changes to lion cropping, no changes to background container shape, no alterations to the lion illustration
- **3 colour versions only** (no other colours):
  1. Gold & Black with Gold Border
  2. White & Black with Black Border
  3. White & Black with White Border
- Face of lion must always be lighter than mane and other features

## Typography

### Font Family
**GT America** by Grilli Type

### Styles & Usage
| Style | Case | Weight | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| Heading | Uppercase | Condensed Black | Main headlines |
| Sub-heading | Uppercase | Condensed Black (smaller) | Section titles |
| Title | Sentence case | Condensed Bold | Labels, slide titles |
| Body | Sentence case | Regular | Body copy, longer text |

### Key Rules
- **Condensed** is the primary style used across product and marketing
- **Regular** used for body text — easier to read in larger chunks
- Headings always in **uppercase**
- Body and titles in **sentence case**

## Presentation Style

_Based on: MBR - BETMGM Brazil | Feb 26 Results_

### Slide Types & Structure

| Slide Type | Background | Layout |
|---|---|---|
| Cover | Dark charcoal + full-bleed hero image | Logo + flag top-left, title bottom-left |
| Section divider | Dark charcoal | Large title bottom-left, gold rule line below |
| Executive Summary | Dark charcoal | Title top-left, body text + coloured bullet highlights |
| Content / Data | White or light | Title top-left in black, charts fill body |
| KPI Trends | White | 4-quadrant grid of charts |
| P&L / Tables | White | Table left + "Key Insights" panel right |
| Marketing Investment | White | Bar chart top, breakdown table bottom |
| Campaign Recap | Dark charcoal | Diagram + bullet list side by side |

### Colours in Presentations
| Element | Colour |
|---|---|
| Slide background (cover, dividers) | Dark charcoal (~`#2A2A2A`) |
| Content slide background | White |
| Chart bars (primary metric) | Gold (`#B19661`) |
| Positive / above-budget numbers | Gold highlight |
| Negative / below-budget numbers | Pink / red highlight |
| Section rule line | Gold |
| Body text | Black (on white) / White (on dark) |

### Logo Placement in Slides
- **Cover**: Stacked logo top-left (with Brazilian flag)
- **Content slides**: Horizontal logo bottom-left (small)
- **Some slides**: Lion icon only, top-right corner
- Confidentiality notice bottom-right: _"CONFIDENTIAL. ALL RIGHTS RESERVED TO BETMGM."_
- Page number bottom-left

### Chart Style
- Bar charts: gold bars, minimal gridlines, clean labels
- Line charts: multiple coloured lines, clearly labelled
- Delta indicators shown with bracket annotations (e.g., +2%, +31%)
- Chart titles: sentence case, bold, left-aligned
- Axis labels: small, grey

### "Key Insights" Panel Convention
- Gold bold header: **Key Insights**
- Bullet points with coloured inline highlights on key numbers
- Gold = positive / on target
- Pink/red = negative / below target
- Used alongside P&L tables and KPI slides

### Section Divider Convention
- Dark background, near-empty slide
- Section title in large white condensed bold, bottom-left
- Short gold horizontal rule line directly below the title
- No other elements

### Writing Style in Slides
- Titles: sentence case, concise (3–6 words)
- Bullet points: start with context label in bold (e.g., **GGR:**, **VIP Segment Recovery:**), then value
- Numbers always include currency (BRL) and unit ('000 where applicable)
- MoM and YTD always shown together where relevant
